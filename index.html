About the Game

Zoey’s Journey isn’t just about matching candies — it’s a heartwarming game that mixes emotional storytelling with satisfying gameplay. After a painful breakup, Zoey returns to her childhood home to rebuild not only its walls, but also her sense of purpose. With each match-3 puzzle you solve, you unlock stylish décor, heartful memories, and important life choices.
This game is perfect for players who love design games, but crave a meaningful narrative. Follow Zoey’s steps as she heals, grows stronger, and finds new love, all while creating her dream space from scratch.

Emotional Journey from Heartbreak to Hope

Zoey’s story is more than a background — it’s the soul of the game. After her life is upended, she returns to a place filled with childhood memories and unresolved emotions. Every renovation is tied to an emotional breakthrough: discovering old letters, reconnecting with friends, or simply creating something new with her own hands.
Your choices — from furniture to who Zoey reconnects with — shape her path. It’s a narrative about healing, starting over, and finding happiness on your own terms.

Main Characters

Zoey

The heart of the story — a young, creative woman trying to rebuild her life after a tough breakup. Zoey is empathetic, ambitious, and slowly regains her confidence through design, friendship, and unexpected romance.

Liam

The local architect who helps with renovations. Calm, supportive, and mysterious, Liam becomes both a design partner and a possible romantic interest — if you choose to let him in.

Mr. Grumpy

Zoey’s moody cat, who seems to have opinions on everything. Though he never talks, his expressions often reflect the emotional undertones of the story. He’s a small but beloved part of Zoey’s healing journey.

FAQ

  • What makes Zoey’s Journey different from other design games?
    It combines match-3 gameplay with a deep narrative where your choices and designs influence Zoey’s emotional growth.
  • Do I need to be good at puzzles to enjoy the game?
    Not at all! The game is designed to be relaxing, with helpful boosts and clear objectives for every level.
  • Can I decorate every room in the house?
    Yes! You’ll unlock new spaces as you progress, from cozy bedrooms to vibrant gardens — all customizable.
  • Is the game free to play?
    Absolutely. The game is free with optional purchases, so you can enjoy the story at your own pace.
